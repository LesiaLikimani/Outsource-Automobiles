About us

Outsource Automobiles

Turning Car Dreams into Driveways.

Founded in 2018 and headquartered in Karen, Nairobi, Outsource Automobiles is a premier vehicle import and sales company built on integrity, professionalism, and trust. We specialize in sourcing high-quality vehicles from reputable global markets and delivering them seamlessly to our clients in Kenya.

Our Promise At Outsource Automobiles, we believe every client deserves more than just a car—they deserve an experience. With a 100% delivery rate since inception and a proven ability to source exclusive, one-of-a-kind models, we have built a reputation for exceeding expectations.

Why Choose Us

What we do

We proudly serve high-net-worth individuals, executives, and expatriates who value reliability, exclusivity, and professionalism.
Our vision is to be Kenya’s most trusted partner for luxury and specialty vehicles—driving excellence in every transaction.

Vehicle Importation

We source and import vehicles directly from trusted global markets, giving our clients access to both standard and luxury models. With a seamless and transparent process, we handle everything—from selection and inspection to shipping and clearance—so you can focus on enjoying your new car.

Vehicle Sales

Our showroom features a variety of vehicles available locally, carefully selected to meet our high standards of quality and performance. For clients with unique preferences, we also facilitate custom orders, ensuring you get exactly the car you envision.

Aftersales Support

Our relationship with you doesn’t end at delivery. We provide reliable aftersales services including servicing, maintenance coordination, and sourcing of genuine spare parts. This ensures your vehicle remains in top condition for years to come.
